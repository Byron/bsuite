BPT Menu

Most of the options are self explaining, but they will be further described in the preferences section.

QuickHelp: Opens a window with some information about the most important commands and flags.

Online Help: Opens a web browser and starts the online help.

Contact: Send me a mail with critics and comments :)

In general you should differ between your preferences and the options in the other sections like "Bevel". This means that your preferences will be consistent between different sessions of Maya, but won't instantly affect the command if there is an equally named flag (like "Normal is Relative" .

If you want the preferences affect the command, hit "Propagate Preferences".

If you want the preferences reflect the actual settings in the submenu's, hit "Update Preferences".
