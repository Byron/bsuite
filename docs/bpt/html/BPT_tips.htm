Tips&Tricks
You can decrease the stepSize when changing values via the channelbox by holding [Ctrl] and dragging the MiddelMouseButton in the viewport
When beveling, it may happen that the distances of the new edges vary quite a lot. To prevent this, you can turn slideRelative to zero which will recalculate the edge vectors and make them the same lenght.

To make one edge four edges:

  1. Select the edgeLine, and chamfer it.
  2. Select one of those edges which are inside of your chamfer and invoke "smartEdgeRing"
  3. Invoke a "boundary"
  4. Invoke a SolidChamfer

Flatshade all Items:

Currently it is a limitation that the normals will be set to smooth when you change your topology through BPT. Nevertheless you can force flatshaded display by selecting

Shading -> Flat Shade All
