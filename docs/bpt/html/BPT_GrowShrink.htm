Grow and Shrink

Grow will expand the selection and shrink will have the opposite effect
I only implemented my own Version of grow and shrink because the native Maya Commands seem not to support undo. My ones will do.
Please note that these commands will be very slow on meshes with 6000+ polygons, and in these cases I suggest to use maya's native grow and shrink.

ByronsPolyTools -Flag ...
LongName ShortName Description
-growSelection -gro - grows your selection
-shrinkSelection -shr - shrinks your selection
